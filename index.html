About Vintek Solutions

Three Decades of Industrial Project Delivery

Vintek Solutions is a U.S.-based industrial engineering and project management company with three decades of experience supporting owners and developers in planning, coordinating, and delivering manufacturing plants and industrial facilities — across the United States and in international markets.

U.S. Foundation, International ReachHeadquartered in South Dakota, our team delivers industrial projects across the United States and in multiple international markets — including complex factory builds overseas.
Full Project Lifecycle CoverageWe engage at any stage — from feasibility and facility planning through engineering coordination, procurement management, construction supervision, and commissioning support.
Single Point of CoordinationWe serve as a trusted owner's representative, aligning contractors, engineers, equipment vendors, and regulatory requirements under one accountable team throughout the project lifecycle.
What We Do

Core Services

A focused set of industrial engineering and project management services, built around the practical demands of factory construction and facility development.

01
Industrial Engineering
Technical coordination across production systems, manufacturing processes, and utility infrastructure. We translate owner requirements into structured engineering scope and support delivery from design through construction.
02
Plant Design & Facility Planning
Facility layout, production flow planning, structural and utility coordination, and space optimization for new greenfield plants or brownfield expansions — developed to meet operational and regulatory requirements.
03
EPC Project Support
Owner-side EPC coordination covering engineering review, procurement oversight, contractor management, and construction supervision. We protect the owner's interests at every stage of the EPC contract lifecycle.
04
Project Management
Structured project management with defined work packages, schedule controls, budget tracking, and risk management — with clear progress reporting to ownership throughout every phase.
05
Turnkey Factory Solutions
End-to-end factory delivery coordination — from site preparation and civil works through production system installation and production-readiness sign-off. We manage all workstreams on behalf of the owner.
06
Procurement Coordination
Vendor qualification, bid evaluation, equipment sourcing, and purchase order management to keep procurement aligned with project schedules, specifications, and budget parameters.
07
Contractor Coordination
Day-to-day management of general contractors, specialty subcontractors, and engineering firms. We establish clear accountability structures and resolve interface issues before they affect schedule or cost.
08
Commissioning & Start-up
Pre-commissioning planning, system-by-system testing, performance verification, and production start-up support — ensuring facilities are brought online safely and meet specified operational requirements before handover.
09
Facility Modernization
Structured upgrade programs for existing industrial plants — covering capacity expansion, utility system upgrades, safety compliance improvements, and equipment replacement, with live-operations coordination to minimize disruption.
10
Technical Consulting
Independent advisory on project feasibility, technology selection, factory layout, capital cost planning, and operational readiness — grounded in direct factory construction experience rather than theoretical frameworks.
Sectors

Industries We Serve

Our industrial project experience spans multiple manufacturing and production sectors, each with distinct facility requirements, regulatory considerations, and operational demands.

General Manufacturing
Discrete and process manufacturing facilities — including production assembly, component fabrication, and mixed-use industrial plants engineered for operational efficiency and scalability.
Food & Beverage
Food-grade processing and packaging plants with hygienic design principles, cold chain infrastructure, and compliance with applicable U.S. and international food safety standards.
Chemicals & Process
Process plant coordination for specialty chemicals, polymers, and related industries — with close attention to safety engineering, hazardous area classification, and environmental compliance.
Energy & Utilities
Industrial facilities for energy production, storage, and distribution — including on-site power generation, utility infrastructure design, and support for industrial-scale energy projects.
Consumer Goods & FMCG
High-volume consumer product manufacturing with automated production lines, packaging integration, and quality infrastructure designed for demanding output requirements and rapid changeover.
Logistics & Warehousing
Distribution centers and warehousing complexes — engineered for throughput, material handling efficiency, and integration with adjacent production operations.
Methodology

How We Deliver

A structured, phase-gated approach — from initial brief through production handover. Each phase has defined deliverables, clear responsibilities, and documented sign-off before the next stage begins.

01
Consultation
Define project scope, ownership objectives, site constraints, and delivery requirements through structured discovery.
02
Feasibility
Site evaluation, regulatory review, preliminary budgeting, and risk identification — delivered as a formal feasibility and options report.
03
Engineering
Coordination of engineering disciplines — civil, structural, mechanical, process, and electrical — through design development and construction documentation.
04
Procurement
Vendor qualification, bid management, equipment sourcing, and purchase order administration to maintain schedule and specification compliance.
05
Construction
Contractor coordination, site supervision, schedule monitoring, and quality oversight throughout the construction phase.
06
Commissioning
System-by-system testing, performance verification against design parameters, and structured production start-up support.
07
Handover
Documentation transfer, operational training, and a defined post-handover support period for a clean transition to the owner's operations team.
